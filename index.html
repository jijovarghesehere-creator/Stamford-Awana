A Typical Club Night

How Discipleship Works

Every Wednesday evening is broken down into three dynamic segments designed to maximize engagement, build lasting mentorships, and deepen your child's understanding of Scripture.

1

Game Time

High-energy, exclusive circle games that get kids moving! This segment teaches teamwork and sportsmanship while providing a fun, chaotic icebreaker to start the evening off right.

2

Handbook Time

Small group discipleship is the core of our ministry. Trained leaders listen as kids recite the Bible verses they learned at home, answer questions, and build meaningful mentoring relationships.

3

Large Group Time

Kids gather together for engaging worship and interactive Bible lessons. We close the night with an awards ceremony to celebrate the hard work they put into their handbooks each week.
