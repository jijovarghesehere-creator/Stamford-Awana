A Typical Club Routine

What Happens at Club?

Every evening brings structure, moving kids through three distinct environments designed for maximum impact and high-energy community.

📢 Large Group Time

Interactive time filled with weekly Bible messages, age-appropriate lessons, worship music, and celebrating student awards.

🤝 Small Group Time

Close conversational circles where kids work through handbooks, engage in scripture learning, and share prayer requests with leaders.

⚡ Awana Games

Fast-paced, high-energy team games built around the unique Awana circle track. Fun, friendly competition that keeps everyone moving!

Tailored Ministry Programs

Every age level features unique uniforms, reward systems, achievement tokens, and custom-mapped handbooks.

Preschoolers

Cubbies

Nurtures the spiritual potential of kids through highly interactive games, fun puppet modules, creative crafts, and critical foundational scriptural building blocks.

Ages: 3 - 4 Years Old
Early Elementary

Sparks

Ignites curiosity about Jesus through chronological walk-throughs of the Bible from Genesis to Revelation, active memory testing, and high-energy square track games.

Grades: K - 2nd Grade
Upper Elementary

T&T

Truth & Training focuses on cementing relational truths, answering heavy faith questions, and building everyday practical disciplines of grace and personal prayer.

Grades: 3rd - 5th Grade
