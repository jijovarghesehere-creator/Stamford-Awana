Built on the 3B Philosophy

Our targeted curriculum provides children with a rock-solid spiritual foundation designed for a lifetime of active faith.

🤝

Belong

Highly relatable, encouraging small groups where kids form deep bonds with vetted mentors and feel safe, validated, and genuinely welcome.

📖

Believe

Scripture memorization structure that anchors deep truths in young minds, teaching children to believe implicitly in God's promises and word.

🌱

Become

Transforming formal book knowledge into actionable, missional living—shaping children to actively become the hands and feet of Jesus.

Tailored Ministry Programs

Every age level features unique uniforms, reward systems, achievement tokens, and deep age-appropriate content packs.

Preschoolers

Cubbies

Sparks

Ignites curiosity about Jesus through chronological walk-throughs of the Bible from Genesis to Revelation, active memory testing, and high-energy square track games.

T&T
